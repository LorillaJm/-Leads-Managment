## üîç Reading the Data

### Understanding Conversion Rates

**Leads ‚Üí Prospects (Goal: 20%)**

- Above 20%: Good lead quality

**Prospects ‚Üí Closed Deals (Goal: 25%)**

- Above 25%: Strong sales performance
